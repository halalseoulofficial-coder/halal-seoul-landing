The Problem

Sound familiar?

If you're a Muslim in Korea, you know this feeling all too well.

🔍

Everything is in Korean

Every ingredient list is written in Korean — and one mistranslation can mean the difference between Halal and Haram. You shouldn't need a dictionary just to buy groceries.

😰

K-Food with no Halal guide

K-Food is everywhere, but Halal certification is rare and hard to spot. Every trip to the supermarket becomes a stressful gamble.

📍

Hard to find Halal spots

Halal restaurant info is scattered across outdated blogs and group chats — rarely in English, almost never up to date.
