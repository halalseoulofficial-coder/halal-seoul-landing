Where We Started

Sound
familiar?

Staying halal in Korea is harder than it looks. Halal Seoul was built to solve exactly these problems.

🤔

Everything is written in Korean

You pick up a product at a convenience store, but the entire ingredient list is in Korean. Even running it through a translation app doesn't tell you where each ingredient comes from or whether it's halal.

😰

Is this gelatin pork-derived?

Gelatin, carmine, shortening — you can't tell the source just from the name. Googling Korean-specific product information almost never gives a clear answer, so you're left guessing.

⏱️

It takes way too long

Ten minutes just to pick one packet of ramen. The time spent manually searching ingredients for every product you consider buying adds up fast — and it never gets easier.

📍

Where can I eat halal nearby?

Halal restaurant info is scattered across old blog posts and group chats — rarely in English, almost never up to date. Finding a place to eat before Maghrib shouldn't require 20 minutes of searching.

Halal Scanner

Point the camera at a barcode for an instant Halal / Haram / Mashbooh verdict. No barcode? Photograph the ingredient label — AI reads and checks every ingredient for you. Pork derivatives, alcohol, and ambiguous additives flagged immediately.
